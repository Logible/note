## 段落论述方式

### 第一个原因分析

- 定义和解释

- sth1. is sth2.
  - which means that
    - 3个主语一致的简单句
    - When引导的时间状语从句

- sth2
  - a severe global issue
  - a chinese traditional virtue
  - a social pervasive trend
  - an active/negative mentality
  - a hot social subject

1. 什么是追星
   1. Pursing stars is popular trend
   2. among youngsters
   3. which means that
      1. we buy their cds,
      2. we take part in their concerts,
      3. and we learn to sing their songs
   4. when we are still young.

2. 什么是孝敬父母
   1. Respecting parents is a chinese traditional virtue
   2. Which means that
      1. we buy gifts for them,
      2. we wash clothes for them,
      3. and we cook delicious for them
   3. when they are in need of us.

3. 什么是乐观
   1. optimism is a active mentality which means that
      1. one fails to be afraid of frustrations.
      2. one believes things things turned out all right.
      3. and one is brave to face any difficulites.
   2. even when one is through the mill.

### 举例

- 可举小例子

作文中见到复数名词, 可举小例子

I love reading masterpieces such as Pride and prejudice and Little prince.

- 可举大例子

- Tips:
  - 举例都用**一般过去式**
  - 使用拿得准的单词和语法
  - 在单词语法正确基础之上. 单词准确
  - 注意逻辑关系词的正确使用

- for example替换
- although multitudes of cases can support my simple view, the following one is most favorable

- example
- I, the other day, had a high fever because it rained heavily last night. unfortunately, no families or roommates took care of me during holiday. at that moment, a dormitory assistant entered to give me some medicines. then, I recovered the next day. so the story vividly teels us a truth that loving hearts seems like lamp which will become lighter in darkness.

### 原因分析

第二段只需要3个原因分析, 9选3$A_3^3$

最佳2+1

- 若可用中文想到原因分析, 便用以下句型写原因分析

  1. The more, the more
  2. Not only, but also
  3. 同位语
  4. although 引导的让步状语从句
  5. if引导的虚拟语气

  - 如果某句话写出来比较短, 加定语/状语拉长.
  - 加不来, 就用which引导的非限制定语从句或者万能状语拉长

- 若中文想不到, 以下论证方式

  1. 定义和解释
  2. 举例
  3. 名人名言
  4. 数据列举

### 具体原因分析

- furthermore

- as a consequence

## 文章写法

### 图画作文

1. 第一段:
   1. 图画描述+中心总结
   2. 第一二句话: 用主语从句引出描述图画
      1. what is obvious is that
         1. A一幅图一个主体两个动作
            1. in the cartoon, sb(定) ,doing sth次要动作(定、状), is doing sth (主要动作) (定、状)
         2. B一幅图两个主体，两个动作
            1. In the cartoon, Sb1 (定) is doing sth1(定、状) and/but sb2 (定) is doing sth2 (定/状)
         3. C两幅图
            1. In the former picture, Sb1 (定) is doing sth1(定、状) ; and/but in the latter one, sb2 (定) is doing sth2 (定/状)
   3. 第三句话
      1. 引出文章中心过渡语
         1. its symbolic meaning ,subtly conveyed should be taken more seriously —— 文章中心
   4. 第四句话, 中心总结
      1. 被动
      2. as...as...
      3. so...that...(倒装)
      4. 比喻
         1. We should do sth.
         2. We should never do sth.
         3. Sth. is very important
         4. Sth. is very serious
      5. 再用[插入语, 双重否定, 强调]加工

2. 第二段
   1. 第一句话: 引出原因分析的句型
      1. there exist a sea of elements to explain my viewpoint.
   2. 第二句话$A_9^3$

3. 第三段
   1. 第一句文章总结
      1. In conclusion +
      2. 推荐句型
         1. 被动
         2. as...as...
         3. so...that...
         4. 比喻
         5. if引导的虚拟语句
      3. 再用[插入语, 双重否定, 强调]加工
   2. 第二句(具体措施)
      1. 双重否定(个人)
   3. 第三句(具体措施)
      1. 虚拟语气(他人)
   4. 第四句

### 图表作文

1. 第一段:
   1. 描述图表+中心总结
   2. 第一二句话: 用主语从句引出描述图画
      1. what looks beyond dispute is that
         1. 一幅图两个主体，两个动作
            1. In the cartoon, Sb1 (定) is doing sth1(定、状) and/but sb2 (定) is doing sth2 (定/状)
         2. 要么是数量增加/减少
         3. 要么是百分比的sb.做了什么事
   3. 第三句话
      1. 引出文章中心过渡语
         1. its symbolic meaning,subtly conveyed should be taken more seriously —— 文章中心
   4. 第四句
      1. 文章中心,自己写简单句
      2. 再用[插入语, 双重否定, 强调]加工
      3. 越来越来, 趋向于
