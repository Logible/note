$$\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} sin^nxdx = \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} cos^nxdx=\begin{cases}
\displaystyle\frac{n-1}{n}·\frac{n-3}{n-2}···\frac{3}{4}·\frac{1}{2}· \frac{\pi}{2}& ,n为大于0正偶数\\\\
\displaystyle\frac{n-1}{n}·\frac{n-3}{n-2}···\frac{4}{5}·\frac{2}{3}& ,n为大于1正奇数
\end{cases}$$
