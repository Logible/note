   1. $f'_x(x_0,y_0)$与$f'_y(x_0,y_0)$是否都存在

   2. $\displaystyle lim_{\Delta x \to 0, \Delta y \to 0}\frac{[f(x_0+\Delta x,y_0+\Delta y)-f(x_0,y_0)]-[f'_x(x_0,y_0)\Delta x + f'_y(x_0,y_0)\Delta y]}{\sqrt{(\Delta x)^2+(\Delta y)^2}}=0$
      1. $=0$可微
